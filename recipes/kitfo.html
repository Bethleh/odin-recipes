Kitfo

Kitfo is an Ethiopian dish

Asfaw recommends lean cuts of meat, like top round, for raw beef dishes, and Messob grinds the meat for kitfo fresh,

often right when it's ordered. “You can't even grind it and keep it the whole day and serve it in the evening,” he says, because it loses its flavor.
